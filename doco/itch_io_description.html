🌌 Story & Concept

In the dark, metallic corridors of a derelict cargo starship, you must lead your family to safety. But you are not alone. Colossal, mechanical Seekers patrol the background, scanning the walkways with searchlights.

You cannot fight. Your only hope is coordination, shadows, and stealth. Command your family members, utilize their unique strengths, solve mechanical puzzles, and survive the micro-migration.

🎮 Gameplay Features

  • Dynamic Escort Commands — Lead your family as a group or target them individually. Use the Follow command to draw them close, and the Freeze/Hide command to send them scrambling into the nearest cover zones.
  • Proactive Sound Distractions — Throw pebbles into the environment to create noise alerts. The giant Seeker will pause, peer in that direction, or move to investigate, allowing you to slip past unnoticed.
  • Cooperative Puzzles — Use the distinct strengths of your family members to bypass obstacles:
    • Adult: Strong enough to launch the Toddler across wide gaps to activate hard-to-reach buttons.
    • Toddler: Small enough to crawl through air vents and reach enclosed control rooms.
    • Elder: Steady and resourceful — can hack terminals to overload and stun the Seeker.
  • Lurking Giant AI — The Seekers feature realistic hearing and sight. They sweep their searchlights vertically across upper and lower walkways. If you are spotted, the warning sensor flashes red — slip behind cover before it completes, or face a relentless chase!

💡 Tips

  1. Watch the Spotlight — The Seeker sweeps its light between the upper and lower floors. Wait for the beam to swing away before crossing exposed windows.
  2. Scramble for Cover — Double-tap Q to send your whole family sprinting to the nearest cover zone. The Seeker cannot spot them while they are hidden.
  3. Sound Travels — Shouting commands when the Seeker is nearby will alert it. Check its position before issuing orders.
  4. Use Distractions — If the Seeker is blocking a vital pathway, throw a pebble (C) to the far side of the map to draw its attention away.
