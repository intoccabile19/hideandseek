# HIDE & SEEK: MICRO-MIGRATION
*An intense sci-fi stealth-puzzle escort adventure.*

---

## 🌌 Story & Concept
In the dark, metallic corridors of a derelict cargo starship, you must lead your family to safety. But you are not alone. A colossal, mechanical **Seeker** patrols the background, scanning the walkways with its searchlight. 

You cannot fight. Your only hope is coordination, shadows, and stealth. Command your family members, utilize their unique strengths, solve mechanical puzzles, and survive the micro-migration.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay Features

* **Dynamic ESCORT Commands**: Lead your family as a group or target them individually. Use the **Follow** command to draw them close, and the **Freeze/Hide** command to send them scrambling into the nearest cover zones.
* **Proactive Sound Distractions**: Throw pebbles into the environment to create noise alerts. The giant Seeker will pause, peer in that direction, or move to investigate, allowing you to slip past unnoticed.
* **Cooperative Puzzle Puzzles**: Use the distinct strengths of your family members to bypass obstacles:
  * **Adult**: Strong enough to launch the Toddler across wide gaps to activate hard-to-reach buttons.
  * **Toddler**: Small enough to crawl through air vents and reach enclosed control rooms.
  * **Elder**: Sturdy and steady, helping you keep the group organized.
* **Lurking Giant AI**: The Seeker features realistic hearing and sight. It sweeps its searchlight vertically across upper and lower walkways. If you are spotted, its warning sensor flashes red. Slip behind cover before the warning completes, or face a relentless chase!

## ⌨️ Controls & Input

| Action | Keyboard Bind |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Move Left / Right** | `A` / `D` or `Arrow Keys` |
| **Jump** | `Spacebar` |
| **Pebble Throw (Distraction)** | `C` |
| **Interact / Activate** | `F` |
| **Family: Follow Command** | `E` |
| **Family: Freeze & Hide** | `Q` |
| **Toddler Ledge Throw** (As Adult) | `S` (when standing in launcher) |
| **Pause Game** | `ESC` |

### Family Targeting Selectors
* Press `1`: Target **ALL** family members.
* Press `2`: Target the **Toddler** only.
* Press `3`: Target the **Elder** only.
* Press `4`: Target the **Adult** only.

## 💡 Quick Tips for Players

1. **Watch the Spotlight Gaze**: The Seeker sweeps its light vertically between the upper and lower floors. Time your runs and wait for the light to sweep to the opposite floor before crossing windows.
2. **Scramble for Cover**: When you shout **Freeze/Hide (`Q`)**, the family members will automatically find and sprint to the nearest cover zones (crates, medical tubes, and consoles). The Seeker cannot spot them when they are hidden.
3. **Sound travels**: Shouting commands when the Seeker is close will alert it. Stand far away or check the Seeker's position before commanding your family.
4. **Use Distractions**: If the Seeker is blocking a vital pathway, throw a pebble (`C`) to the opposite side of the map to draw its attention away.
